# SEO Interpretation Thresholds

## Bounce Rate

| Rating | Range | Context |
|--------|-------|---------|
| Excellent | < 30% | High engagement |
| Good | 30-50% | Normal for most content |
| Concerning | 50-70% | Review content quality |
| Poor | > 70% | Intent mismatch or UX issue |

**Page type context**:
- Blog posts: 60-70% can be normal
- Landing pages: aim for < 50%
- Product/pricing: aim for < 40%

## Visit Duration

| Rating | Range | Meaning |
|--------|-------|---------|
| Excellent | > 3 min | Deep engagement |
| Good | 1-3 min | Normal consumption |
| Concerning | 30-60 sec | Skimming |
| Poor | < 30 sec | Immediate bounce |

## Traffic Changes (Week-over-Week)

| Severity | Change | Action |
|----------|--------|--------|
| Normal | ±15% | Monitor |
| Notable | ±15-30% | Investigate sources/pages |
| Significant | ±30-50% | Root cause analysis |
| Critical | > ±50% | Immediate investigation |

## Traffic Source Quality

| Grade | Bounce Rate | Duration | Verdict |
|-------|-------------|----------|---------|
| A | < 30% | > 3 min | Excellent - scale up |
| B | 30-50% | 1-3 min | Good quality |
| C | 50-70% | 30-60 sec | Review targeting |
| D/F | > 70% | < 30 sec | Poor - review/stop |

## Investigation Triggers

- Page with >50 visitors/week AND >80% bounce = priority review
- Entry page with >100 visitors AND >70% bounce = SEO concern
- Any page with <10 sec avg time = likely broken/misleading
- Traffic drop >30% on key page = immediate investigation

## Content Decay Indicators

| Drop | Severity | Typical Cause |
|------|----------|---------------|
| 30-50% | High | Ranking loss, seasonal |
| > 50% | Critical | Major algorithm change, competitor |

## Quick Diagnosis Patterns

**High bounce + short time**: Wrong audience or broken UX
**High bounce + long time**: Content satisfied need (single-page intent)
**Low bounce + short time**: Navigating but not engaging
**Low bounce + long time**: Excellent - replicate pattern
